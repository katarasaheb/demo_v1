Canada's Grocery System is Broken

Canadian grocery shopping is expensive, inefficient, and monopolized.

  • Monopolized Market: 5 major companies control Canada's grocery market, contributing to high prices.
  • Outdated Supply Chains: Multi-tiered systems lead to markups of 5x–7x.
  • Limited Consumer Choice: Limited variety and high costs due to lack of competition.
